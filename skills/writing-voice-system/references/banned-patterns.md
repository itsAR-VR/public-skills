## Formatting Tells

- em dashes
- excessive bold text
- a header before every paragraph
- three-adjective chains
- arrow lists in body copy

## Structural Bans

- Do not start with `In today’s...`
- Do not use trailing `-ing` significance claims that fake depth
- Do not use `It’s not X, it’s Y` constructions
- Do not use `No X. No Y. Just Z.`
- Do not turn reflection into sales copy
- Do not end with a motivational slogan

## Replacement Strategy

When a sentence trips this filter:

1. Replace abstract claims with a memory, scene, object, place, or named person.
2. Replace fake authority with either a real source or no authority claim at all.
3. Replace puffery with plain language.
4. Replace a polished transition with the next actual thought.
5. Replace generic emphasis with sentence rhythm.

## Hormozi Humanizer Additions

Also run the `hormozi-humanizer` pass for memos, posts, client notes, and
founder writing. In addition to the list above, remove:

- neat-bow endings that could apply to any company
