# Hormozi Skill: Lead Generation

Use when: you need a predictable inbound/outbound lead pipeline and want to prioritize highest-leverage channels.

## Core principles
- Leads are generated through a small set of repeatable methods; master those before expanding.
- Pair traffic with **offers/lead magnets**; leads alone are insufficient.
- Use channel fit by audience temperature and communication format.
- Content should follow **Hook → Retain → Reward**.
- Build second-order growth through **referrals, employees, affiliates/partners**.

## Key frameworks
### 1) The Core Four Advertising Methods
- **1-to-1 Warm Audience = Warm Outreach**
- **1-to-many Warm Audience = Post Free Content**
- **1-to-1 Cold Audience = Cold Outreach**
- **1-to-many Cold Audience = Paid Ads**

### 2) Lead Magnet Creation (7-step concept in source)
Use this practical version:
1. Pick one painful, immediate problem.
2. Offer a fast win artifact (checklist/template/script/calculator).
3. Make consumption short and specific.
4. Tie magnet directly to paid/core offer.
5. Add clear CTA to next step (call/demo/trial).
6. Distribute via Core Four.
7. Improve from conversion/engagement data.

### 3) Referrals as Lead Getters
From source emphasis:
- Referrals grow value and can improve LTV profile.
- Ask at high-trust moments (purchase, milestone, win).
- Use one-sided, two-sided, event-based, ongoing, or unlockable referral structures.

## Practical templates
### Template A: Core Four Planner
- Warm outreach list: `[former leads/customers/network]`
- Free content topics: `[pain1, pain2, pain3]`
- Cold outreach ICP + script: `[avatar + opener + CTA]`
- Paid ad hypothesis: `[hook + audience + offer]`

### Template B: Hook-Retain-Reward Content Script
- Hook: `If you’re [avatar] and struggling with [pain], read this.`
- Retain: `Most people fail because [mistake]. Here’s the 3-step fix.`
- Reward: `Use this [tool/checklist]. If you want help implementing, [CTA].`

### Template C: Referral Ask
`Quick favor: who are 1-2 people like you who’d benefit from [result]? I’ll give them [specific benefit], and I’ll send you [thank-you incentive].`

## Examples
1. Warm outreach sprint:
- 50 past contacts, personalized DM/email, same core offer, 7-day follow-up cadence.

2. Lead magnet bridge:
- Free “5-day onboarding checklist” → booked setup call for paid implementation.

3. Affiliate motion:
- Partner promotes lead magnet to their list; you nurture to core offer.
