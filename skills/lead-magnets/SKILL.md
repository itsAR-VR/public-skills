# Lead Magnets

You are an expert in lead magnet strategy. Your goal is to help plan lead magnets that capture emails, generate qualified leads, and naturally lead to product adoption.

## Before Planning

**Check for product marketing context first:**
If `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` exists (or `.claude/product-marketing-context.md` in older setups), read it before asking questions. Use that context and only ask for information not already covered or specific to this task.

Gather this context (ask if not provided):

### 1. Business Context
- What does the company do?
- Who is the ideal customer?
- What problems does your product solve?

### 2. Current Lead Generation
- How do you currently capture leads?
- What lead magnets or offers do you have?
- What is your current conversion rate on email capture?

### 3. Content Assets
- What existing content could be repurposed? (blog posts, guides, data)
- What expertise can you package?
- What templates or tools do you use internally?

### 4. Goals
- Primary goal: email list growth, lead quality, product education?
- Target audience stage: awareness, consideration, or decision?
- Timeline and resource constraints?

## Lead Magnet Scoring Framework

Use this to quickly rank competing lead magnet ideas before committing resources.

Score each idea 1-3 on each dimension, then pick the highest total:

| Dimension | 1 (Weak) | 2 (Solid) | 3 (Strong) |
|-----------|----------|-----------|------------|
| **Problem specificity** | Broad topic | Specific niche | Solves one precise pain |
| **Speed to value** | Hours to use | 30 min to use | Immediate aha moment |
| **Path to product** | Tangential | Related | Directly demonstrates product value |
| **Reuse potential** | One-time read | Reference material | Template/tool used repeatedly |
| **Creation effort** | 2+ weeks | 3-5 days | 1-2 days |
| **Uniqueness** | Generic (exists everywhere) | Differentiated | Only you can make this |

**Target:** 15+/18 = build it. 10-14 = refine the idea. <10 = pick a different angle.

## Lead Magnet Principles

### 1. Solve a Specific Problem
- Address one clear pain point, not a broad topic
- "How to write cold emails that get replies" > "Marketing guide"

### 2. Match the Buyer Stage
- Awareness leads need education
- Consideration leads need comparison and evaluation
- Decision leads need implementation help

### 3. High Perceived Value, Low Time Investment
- Should look like it is worth paying for
- Consumable in under 30 minutes (ideally under 10)
- Immediate, actionable takeaway

### 4. Natural Path to Product
- Solves a problem your product also solves
- Creates awareness of a gap your product fills
- Demonstrates your expertise in the space

### 5. Easy to Consume
- One clear format (do not mix ebook + video + spreadsheet)
- Works on mobile
- No special software required

## Matching Lead Magnets to Buyer Stage

### Awareness Stage
Goal: Educate on the problem. Attract people who do not know you yet.

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Checklist | "10-Point Website Audit Checklist" |
| Cheat sheet | "SEO Cheat Sheet for Beginners" |
| Ebook/guide | "The Complete Guide to Email Marketing" |
| Quiz | "What Type of Marketer Are You?" |

### Consideration Stage
Goal: Help evaluate solutions. Build trust and demonstrate expertise.

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Comparison template | "CRM Comparison Spreadsheet" |
| Assessment | "Marketing Maturity Assessment" |
| Case study collection | "5 Companies That 3x'd Their Pipeline" |
| Webinar | "How to Choose the Right Analytics Tool" |

### Decision Stage
Goal: Help implement. Remove friction to purchase.

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Template | "Ready-to-Use Sales Email Templates" |
| Free trial | "14-Day Free Trial" |
| Implementation guide | "Migration Checklist: Switch in 30 Minutes" |
| ROI calculator | "Calculate Your Savings" (see free-tool-strategy) |

## Universal Lead Magnet Creation Checklist

Use this checklist when building any lead magnet format:

### Phase 1: Concept (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Identify the one specific pain point it solves
- [ ] Confirm it matches buyer stage (awareness/consideration/decision)
- [ ] Score the idea using the Scoring Framework above (target 15+/18)
- [ ] Verify the natural path to product is clear
- [ ] Write the headline using the formula for your chosen format

### Phase 2: Structure (1-4 hours)
- [ ] Write a one-paragraph "what's inside" description
- [ ] Outline key sections/components (5-10 items for lists; 3-7 sections for guides)
- [ ] Decide what is gated vs. preview (see Gating Strategy)
- [ ] Draft the 3-5 bullet points for the landing page ("What You'll Get")

### Phase 3: Creation (varies by format)
- [ ] Create the content (use copywriting skill for the writing)
- [ ] Add design elements (cover mockup, brand colors, clean layout)
- [ ] Ensure mobile-readable (no tiny fonts, no wide tables)
- [ ] Add your logo/URL for passive brand reinforcement

### Phase 4: Delivery Setup
- [ ] Set up opt-in form (use form-cro skill)
- [ ] Create landing page (use page-cro skill)
- [ ] Configure confirmation + delivery email (instant download link OR email attachment)
- [ ] Build thank-you page with next-step CTA
- [ ] Connect to email sequence (use email-sequence skill)
- [ ] Test the full opt-in to delivery flow end-to-end

### Phase 5: Promotion
- [ ] Add blog CTAs/content upgrades on relevant posts
- [ ] Set up popup/exit-intent trigger (use popup-cro skill)
- [ ] Post social media teasers
- [ ] Configure paid promotion if applicable (use paid-ads skill)

## Gating Strategy

### Gating Options

| Approach | When to Use | Trade-off | Typical Conversion |
|----------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Full gate** | High-value content, bottom-funnel | Max capture, lower reach | 20-40% (warm) / 5-15% (cold) |
| **Partial gate** | Preview + full version | Balance of reach and capture | 15-30% |
| **Ungated + optional** | Top-funnel education | Max reach, lower capture | 2-8% (email ask) |
| **Content upgrade** | Blog post + bonus | Contextual, high-intent | 30-60% (post-specific) |

### What to Ask For

- **Email only** — highest conversion, lowest friction
- **Email + name** — enables personalization, slight friction increase (~5-10% drop)
- **Email + company/role** — better lead qualification, more friction (~15-25% drop)
- **Multi-field** — only for high-value offers (webinars, demos); expect 30-50% drop vs email-only

Rule of thumb: Ask for the minimum needed. Every extra field reduces conversion by 5-10%.

### How to Frame the Exchange

- Make the value obvious: "Get the full 25-page guide free"
- Show a preview: table of contents, first page, sample results
- Add social proof: "Downloaded by 5,000+ marketers"
- Reduce risk: "No spam. Unsubscribe anytime."

## Landing Page and Delivery

### Landing Page Structure

1. **Headline** — Clear benefit: what they will get and why it matters
2. **Preview/mockup** — Visual of the lead magnet (cover, screenshot, sample page)
3. **What's inside** — 3-5 bullet points of key takeaways
4. **Social proof** — Download count, testimonials, logos
5. **Form** — Minimal fields, clear CTA button
6. **FAQ** — Address hesitations (Is it really free? What format?)

**For landing page optimization**: See page-cro skill

### Delivery Methods

| Method | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Instant download** | Immediate gratification | No email verification |
| **Email delivery** | Verifies email, starts relationship | Slight delay |
| **Thank you page + email** | Best of both—instant access + email copy | Slightly more complex |
| **Drip delivery** | Builds habit, multiple touchpoints | Only for courses/series |

### Thank You Page Optimization

Do not waste the thank you page. After they have converted:
- Confirm delivery ("Check your inbox")
- Offer a next step (book a demo, start trial, join community)
- Share on social (pre-written tweet/post)
- Recommend related content

## Promotion and Distribution

### Channel Decision Matrix

Pick your priority channels based on your situation:

| Situation | Priority Channels | Skip |
|-----------|------------------|------|
| B2B, enterprise audience | LinkedIn ads + cold email + partner webinars | TikTok, Instagram Reels |
| B2C, broad consumer | Instagram/Facebook ads + quiz/viral format | LinkedIn, cold email |
| Content-heavy blog (10k+ monthly) | Content upgrades + exit-intent popup | Paid ads until organic optimized |
| Small list (<500 subs), limited budget | Content upgrades + social organic + SEO | Paid ads |
| Existing audience (social/community) | Organic posts + story links + bio CTA | Paid ads until organic validated |
| No existing audience, paid budget | Facebook/Instagram lead ads + landing page | Partner co-promotion |
| High-ACV B2B ($5k+) | LinkedIn lead gen + cold outreach | Broad Facebook ads |

### Blog CTAs and Content Upgrades

- Add relevant CTAs within blog posts (inline, end-of-post)
- Create post-specific content upgrades (bonus checklist for a how-to post)
- Content upgrades convert 2-5x better than generic sidebar CTAs

### Exit-Intent and Popups

- Trigger on exit intent or scroll depth
- Match the popup offer to the page content
- See popup-cro for implementation

### Social Media

- Share snippets and teasers from the lead magnet
- Create carousel posts from key points
- Use the lead magnet as the CTA in your bio/profile
- See social-content for social strategy

### Paid Promotion

- Facebook/Instagram lead ads for top-funnel lead magnets
- Google Ads for high-intent lead magnets (templates, tools)
- LinkedIn for B2B lead magnets
- Retarget blog visitors with lead magnet ads
- See paid-ads for campaign strategy

### Partner Co-Promotion

- Cross-promote with complementary brands
- Guest webinars with partner audiences
- Include in partner newsletters
- Bundle in resource collections

## Measuring Success

### Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Tells You | Benchmark |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Landing page conversion rate** | Offer attractiveness | 20-40% (warm traffic), 5-15% (cold) |
| **Cost per lead** | Acquisition efficiency | Varies by channel and industry |
| **Lead-to-customer rate** | Lead quality | 1-5% (B2B), varies widely |
| **Email engagement** | Content relevance | 30-50% open, 2-5% click |
| **Time to conversion** | Nurture effectiveness | Track by lead magnet source |

**For detailed benchmarks by format and industry**: See [references/benchmarks.md](references/benchmarks.md)

### Lead Quality Scoring

After 30 days, score each lead magnet by lead quality:

| Signal | Weight | Measure |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Email open rate (first 3 emails) | 3x | >40% = high quality audience |
| Click-through rate | 3x | >5% = engaged leads |
| Trial/demo conversion | 5x | Compare by lead magnet source |
| Unsubscribe rate after delivery | -2x | <2% = good fit |
| ICP match rate (if enriched) | 4x | % matching title/company size/industry |

If a lead magnet brings email opens but no trials or demos, it is attracting the wrong audience. Reconsider the topic, format, or placement.

### A/B Testing Ideas

- **Headline**: Benefit-focused vs. curiosity-driven
- **Format**: Checklist vs. guide on same topic
- **Gate level**: Full gate vs. partial preview
- **Form fields**: Email-only vs. email + name
- **CTA copy**: "Download Free Guide" vs. "Get Your Copy"
- **Delivery**: Instant download vs. email delivery

## Common Lead Magnet Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Too broad** | "The Ultimate Marketing Guide" attracts everyone and converts no one | Narrow to one specific tactic for one specific role |
| **Wrong stage match** | Bottom-funnel template promoted via top-funnel blog post | Match the buyer stage of your traffic source to the lead magnet offer |
| **No natural path to product** | Ebook on general topic does not mention your product category | Ensure the lead magnet solves a problem your product also solves, then bridge it |
| **Gating low-value content** | PDF with info freely available on Google — no reason to opt in | If it is Googleable, it is not gate-worthy; add unique data, templates, or tools |
| **No delivery email sequence** | Lead downloads and never hears from you again | Always wire a post-download email sequence (minimum 3 emails) |
| **Ugly or hard-to-read design** | Amateur PDF = low perceived value = unsubscribe | Budget 2-4 hours for design; use Canva/Notion for clean, professional output |
| **Thank you page is a dead end** | Missed chance to convert the warmest possible visitor | Add a clear next-step CTA (demo, trial, related content) on every thank you page |
| **No content upgrade variants** | One lead magnet for all traffic | Create post-specific upgrades for top 3-5 blog posts; expect 2-5x conversion lift |
