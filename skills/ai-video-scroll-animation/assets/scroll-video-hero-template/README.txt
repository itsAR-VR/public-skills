AI Video Scroll Hero — Drop-in Template
========================================

Files:
  index.html — Full hero section with video scrub + image sequence modes

To use:
1. Drop index.html into your project
2. Replace /videos/hero-web.mp4 with your Higgsfield-generated + ffmpeg-converted clip
3. Or set USE_IMAGE_SEQUENCE = true and point FRAME_PATH to your extracted frames
4. Adjust CSS variables (hero-section height, font sizes, colors) to match your brand
5. Update the headline/subhead/CTA text
6. Run your dev server and verify in browser

See SKILL.md and references/ for full generation + conversion pipeline.
