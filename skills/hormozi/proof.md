# Hormozi Skill: Proof

Use when: prospects doubt claims, ads underperform due to trust gaps, or testimonials feel weak/generic.

## Core principles
- Proof quality often matters more than claim quality.
- Strong proof is specific, credible, and matched to the prospect identity.
- “Show” beats “tell.”
- Third-party and verifiable proof outperform self-assertion.
- Build proof collection into customer journey, not ad-hoc.

## Key frameworks
### 1) Proof Checklist (13 factors from source)
Priority ladder includes:
- In-Person > Virtual
- Live > Recorded
- Raw > Processed
- Show > Tell
- Other People Saying Your Stuff’s Great > You Saying It
- Identical to Them > Opposite of Them
- Personal > Generic
- Big Results > Small Results > No Results
- Newer Proof > Older Proof
- More Proof > Less Proof
- Third Party Verification > Zero Verification
- Proof With Numbers > Without Numbers
- Metaphors > Technical Jargon

### 2) Testimonial structure guidance
Extract from examples:
- Before state (specific pain/context)
- Catalyst/decision moment
- Mechanism/process used
- After state (specific measurable result)
- Identity match (same avatar attributes as target prospect)

### 3) Proof Ops Loop
1. Capture moments live and raw when possible.
2. Prompt customers with structured questions.
3. Tag proof assets by avatar, pain, result, channel.
4. Deploy closest-match proof near offer/CTA.
5. Refresh with newer assets regularly.

## Practical templates
### Template A: Testimonial prompt
`What was happening before? What specific pain pushed you to act? What did you try with us? What exact result did you get (numbers/time)? What can you do now that you couldn’t before?`

### Template B: Proof asset tag schema
- Avatar: `[ ]`
- Pain: `[ ]`
- Result metric: `[ ]`
- Format: `[live/raw/recorded/etc.]`
- Verification: `[review platform / screenshot / third-party]`

### Template C: Claim-to-proof mapping
- Claim: `[We help X get Y]`
- Matching proof: `[case #, same avatar, number-backed]`
- Placement: `[landing page section / ad / sales call]`

## Examples
1. Live webinar:
- Bring customer live for transformation story instead of only playing edited montage.

2. Service ad:
- Replace generic “great service” quote with raw phone video + exact numeric outcome.

3. Multi-avatar business:
- Maintain separate proof banks for each demographic/segment to improve relevance.
