# App Category Templates

Category-specific slide prompts and hook formulas.

## Home / Interior Design
**Base prompt style:** iPhone photo of a [specific room], [style], [furniture details]. Realistic lighting, natural colors. No text, no watermarks.

**Hook formulas:**
- "My [person] said I can't change anything... challenge accepted"
- "POV: AI redesigns your [room] in 10 seconds"
- "Same room, different energy"
- "This is why your [room] looks cheap (and how to fix it for free)"

**Consistency anchors:** Same window placement, same sofa, same flooring in every prompt.

## Beauty / Skincare / Cosmetic
**Base prompt style:** Close-up iPhone selfie, [skin type], [lighting style], [background]. Portrait mode blur, natural skin texture. No filters, no text.

**Hook formulas:**
- "POV: you finally find [product] that actually works"
- "I've been using [product] for [time] and..."
- "My [friend/partner] didn't recognize me after [product]"
- "Stop doing this to your skin (it's making it worse)"

**Consistency anchors:** Same face angle, same lighting direction, same background.

## Fitness / Health
**Base prompt style:** iPhone photo of [person/scene], [gym/home setting], [workout context]. Natural sweat, realistic gym lighting. No posed perfection.

**Hook formulas:**
- "[Time period] transformation using just [product/method]"
- "My trainer said this was impossible"
- "The workout nobody talks about that actually works"
- "POV: you stop making excuses and just start"

**Consistency anchors:** Same gym/location, same person build, same clothing style.

## Productivity / Tech
**Base prompt style:** iPhone screenshot or desk setup photo, [clean/messy contrast], [device in use]. Realistic screen content, natural desk lighting.

**Hook formulas:**
- "This app replaced [expensive tool] for me"
- "I automated [task] and now I have [time] back"
- "Everyone's using [popular tool] but [your tool] does it better"
- "My morning routine before vs after [product]"

## Food / Recipe
**Base prompt style:** iPhone overhead or 45-degree food photo, [dish], [setting/plate style]. Natural kitchen lighting, steam/texture visible. No professional staging.

**Hook formulas:**
- "I can't believe this only took [time]"
- "My [person] asked if I ordered this from a restaurant"
- "The recipe that made me stop ordering takeout"
- "POV: you actually learn to cook [dish] from scratch"

**Consistency anchors:** Same kitchen counter/table, same plate style, same lighting angle.

## General Rules (All Categories)

1. Always use **portrait 1024x1536** (fills TikTok screen)
2. Include "iPhone photo" and "realistic lighting" in every prompt
3. Lock the scene/environment across all 6 slides
4. Add everyday imperfections — perfect = obviously AI
5. Specify exact details — vague prompts = generic results
6. Before/after slides: "before" should be modern but tired, NOT ancient/destroyed
